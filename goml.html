Get Off My Lawn (2023)

Protect your lawn by spraying intruders with your hose, send your dog to get the toys off of your lawn, keep yourself rocking in your chair to stay awake, and keep drinking your beverage to keep your energy up. This project is using an alt-controller made of a rocking chair and other elements to play the game.

  • About the Game

    Made in Unreal
    Team Size: 10
    Timeline: about 4 months
    Worked On: Game Design, Game Audio

    Nominated for IndieCade 2023 Live Action Award! Also asked to be a part of IndieCade 2023 Night Games!

    This is an Alt-Ctrl game for my second semester graduate course. On the project, I helped work on design along with the rest of the team. This includes a dog mechanic that we ultimately had to cut. I also worked on the sound design for the game, including all the music and SFX for the game. The main menu page, cinematic, and tutorial page were also programmed and designed by me - not including the art.
