    As a Audio Engineer/Designer

    I have filled the role as Audio Engineer/Designer. I used Cakewalk for my DAW and have messed around with a bunch of different sounds and music. I spent a lot of my time on our music system blueprint. I wanted to make a system that allowed the player to take however long they need in the game. Due to this, I thought one long track would be a bad route to take - I didn't want it to obviously loop. I have made short 20 measure tracks that flow into each other by keeping the baseline the same. Then, my music system blueprint will pick a random track out of the ones it's given to play. My prototype audio and some of music system can be seen and played below.
    I was able to record the player efforts with a member of the team, and implement those into our game. I set up all of the player SFX within the animation notifies. The only exception being some looping sounds and special sounds that are being called from the cpp code.
    I had a lot of fun making the music system blueprint and am still tweaking it to make it better. I used the Quartz system that Unreal has, and it has worked very good for us so far.
