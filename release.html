  • About the Game

    Made in Unreal
    Team Size: 5
    Timeline: about 3 weeks
    Worked On: Game Design, Game Programming

    The team disscused as a whole what our direction was going to be, given the restriction of it being a serious game. We decided on a rage room and a music therepy room.

    I helped design and programmed the music orbs. After the player destroys what they would like in the glass rage room, they move on to the soothing music room. Being in VR, the player is able to reach out and touch the orbs. Doing so plays a note, and they can make their own melody to the background music by touching many of the orbs in whatever pattern they like.
